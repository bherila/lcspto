Liberty Corner School Room Parents

LCS PTO is looking for room parents for the upcoming school year.

As room parent, your responsibilities will be as follows:

  • Attend the Room Parent meeting on Wednesday, September 15th at 8:15 a.m.
  • Attend Back to School Night on Monday, September 20 (K-2) or Tuesday, September 21 (3-5) and collect PTO dues, teacher gift money and specials teacher gift money.
  • At least one room parent must come to every PTO meeting.
  • Work with the teacher throughout the school year to plan parties, arrange chaperones for field trips and help with other events at the teacher's request
  • Participate in Positive Action by contacting parents and reminding them to vote in the April election

We will give priority to those parents who have not yet had an opportunity to be room parent for a particular child.

ALL communication to Room Parents will be via e-mail, so if you sign up to volunteer be sure you check your e-mail FREQUENTLY.
