Our Mission

  • To disseminate information to parents via news blasts, newsletters and our website;
  • To foster community within our school through various family and social events;
  • To raise funds for programs to augment the school's curriculum.

There are many opportunities to get involved in the PTO; from volunteering a half hour of your time at an event, to chairing a committee, to serving on the PTO Board. All are welcome and no experience is necessary!

Remember: Those who can, do...those who can do more volunteer!
